Moon

The Moon is Earth’s loyal sidekick, a rocky satellite that orbits us every 27 days and quietly controls tides, stabilizes our planet’s tilt, and inspires countless songs and myths. It doesn’t produce its own light, but reflects the Sun’s glow—proving you don’t have to shine on your own to steal the spotlight. Basically, it’s a cosmic nightlight with a powerful influence and excellent timing.
